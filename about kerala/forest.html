A Journey through the Forest

Bonacaud

Nestled on the foothills of the Agasthya hills of Western Ghats, Bonacaud in Thiruvananthapuram district is a pristine, green haven that presents a multitude of experiences.

Peechi-Vazhani Wildlife Sanctuary, Thrissur

The Peechi-Vazhani Wildlife Sanctuary in Thrissur district, Kerala is established in 1958. The Sanctuary is part of the Palapilli-Nelliampathy Forests.

Muthanga Wildlife Sanctuary

Muthanga in Sulthan Bathery, Wayanad district of Kerala is an ideal destination to watch herds of wild elephants and other wildlife of Kerala.

Silent Valley National Park

Silent Valley National Park, situated at Mannarkkad in Palakkad district, is the core of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. Silent Valley offers some of the best trekking.

Sankhili Forest

The Sankhili forest (valley), well-known for its varied flora and fauna, is a hotspot in the Western Ghats. It comes under the Kulathupuzha forest range in Kollam district.
