Discover the Beauty of Kerala

Cruise in Houseboat

Glide through Kerala's tranquil backwaters on a luxurious houseboat. Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of lush greenery and shimmering waters. For booking and more details, contact DTPCs or Accredited Houseboat Operators.

Walk through the Woods

Take an enchanting walk through Kerala's lush forests. Explore ecotourism initiatives by the Kerala Forest Department and delve into the heart of nature. Visit: keralatourism.org/ecotourism.

Stay in the Lap of Nature

Escape to Kerala's untouched corners and camp under starlit skies. Discover hidden treasures and experience nature's vibrant soul. Ideal for nature enthusiasts and adventurers.

Kuthampully Handloom Village

Discover Kuthampully, Kerala's renowned handloom hub. Marvel at the intricate craftsmanship of kasavu sarees and experience the timeless tradition of the Devanga community.
