FESTIVALS

Kerala is host to a plethora of traditional festivals that take place throughout the year. A wide number of regions and communities are involved in these celebrations. The State unites to commemorate these great occasions together and the entire place is covered in lights. There are huge processions and massive displays on the street with crowds thronging to soak in the pomp and splendour. Families gather from various corners of the world for these events and giant feasts are held. Festivals are among the best times to visit the State as they encompass what it truly means to be a Keralite.
