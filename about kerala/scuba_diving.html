Dive into the Deep Blue

Kovalam is an internationally renowned beach in God's Own Country. This beach destination offers experiences such as sunbathing, swimming, catamaran cruises, and water sports such as boating, scuba diving, parasailing, surfing, and so on.

Kovalam is a perfect spot to do scuba diving in Kerala.

Discover the magical underwater world with Bond Safari Kovalam. All age groups can take part in this exciting aquatic experience. No diving or scuba experience is needed.
